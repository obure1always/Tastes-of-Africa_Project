Jollof Rice

A West African classic dish

Description

Jollof Rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and various spices. It's known for its vibrant red color and rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 large tomatoes, blended
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers, blended
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice until the water runs clear and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and saut√© onions until translucent.
  3. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  4. Add the blended tomatoes and peppers, tomato paste, and spices.
  5. Cook the mixture for 10-15 minutes until it thickens.
  6. Add the rice and stir to coat with the sauce.
  7. Add water (1:1 ratio with rice) and bring to a boil.
  8. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until rice is cooked.
  9. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Tips

  • Use long-grain rice for the best results
  • Don't stir the rice while it's cooking to prevent it from becoming mushy
  • Add a little more water if the rice seems too dry
