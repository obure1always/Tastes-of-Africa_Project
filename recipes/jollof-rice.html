Jollof Rice

A beloved West African dish known for its rich tomato flavor and aromatic spices.

Prep Time

30 minutes

Cook Time

45 minutes

Servings

6-8 people

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 4 large tomatoes, blended
  • 2 red bell peppers, blended
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 stock cubes
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice until the water runs clear and set aside.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  3. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  4. Add the minced garlic and cook for 1 minute.
  5. Pour in the blended tomatoes and peppers, and cook for 10 minutes.
  6. Add the tomato paste, thyme, bay leaves, curry powder, white pepper, and stock cubes.
  7. Cook the mixture for another 10 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Add the rinsed rice and stir to combine with the sauce.
  9. Add enough water to cover the rice (about 2 cups).
  10. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover the pot.
  11. Cook for 30-35 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  12. Remove from heat and let it rest for 5 minutes before serving.

Tips

  • For the best results, use parboiled rice.
  • Don't stir the rice too much while it's cooking to prevent it from becoming mushy.
  • You can add vegetables like carrots and peas for extra nutrition.
  • Serve with fried plantains or grilled chicken for a complete meal.
