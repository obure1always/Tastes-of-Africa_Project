Jollof Rice

A beloved West African dish known for its vibrant red color and rich flavor. This one-pot rice dish is a staple at celebrations and family gatherings.

View Recipe
Cooking Instructions
  1. Blend tomatoes, red peppers, and onions to make a puree
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and sauté onions
  3. Add tomato puree and cook until reduced
  4. Add rice, stock, and seasonings
  5. Cook on low heat until rice is tender

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 350
Protein 6g
Carbohydrates 65g
Fat 8g

Origin: Nigeria

Injera

A traditional Ethiopian flatbread with a unique spongy texture and slightly sour taste. This staple food is used as both a plate and utensil in Ethiopian cuisine.

View Recipe
Cooking Instructions
  1. Mix teff flour with water to create a batter
  2. Allow the batter to ferment for 2-3 days
  3. Heat a large flat pan or griddle
  4. Pour the batter in a circular motion
  5. Cook until bubbles form and the edges lift

Ingredients

  • 2 cups teff flour
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda (optional)

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 120
Protein 4g
Carbohydrates 25g
Fat 1g

Origin: Ethiopia
