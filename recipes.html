Jollof Rice

Jollof rice is a beloved West African dish that brings together rice, tomatoes, and a blend of spices. It's a staple at celebrations and family gatherings.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and onions to make a smooth paste
  2. Heat oil in a large pot and saut√© the tomato paste
  3. Add spices and seasonings
  4. Add rice and stir well
  5. Add stock and cook until rice is tender

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 350
Protein 6g
Carbohydrates 70g
Fat 5g

Injera

Injera is a sourdough flatbread that's a staple in Ethiopian cuisine. Its unique texture and slightly sour taste make it perfect for scooping up stews and curries.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Mix teff flour with water to make a batter
  2. Allow the batter to ferment for 2-3 days
  3. Heat a large flat pan
  4. Pour the batter in a circular motion
  5. Cook until bubbles form and edges lift

Ingredients

  • 2 cups teff flour
  • 3 cups water
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 200
Protein 7g
Carbohydrates 40g
Fat 1g

Moroccan Tagine

A traditional Moroccan dish cooked in a special earthenware pot, combining tender meat with aromatic spices and vegetables.

Cooking Instructions
  1. Marinate meat with spices
  2. Layer vegetables in the tagine pot
  3. Add meat and more vegetables
  4. Cook slowly over low heat
  5. Garnish with fresh herbs

Ingredients

  • 1 kg lamb or chicken
  • 2 onions
  • 4 carrots
  • 2 potatoes
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coriander
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Nutritional Information

Nutrient Amount per serving
Calories 450
Protein 35g
Carbohydrates 30g
Fat 20g
